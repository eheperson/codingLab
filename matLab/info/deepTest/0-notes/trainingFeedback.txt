
> Turns of the training window and gives us training status info every 35 epochs 
  when the network is later trained with train :

        net.trainParam.showWindow = False;
        net.trainParam.ShowCommandLine = True;
        net.trainParam.show = 35;

!! Sometimes it is convenient to disable all training displays.

showWindow       : Allows usto specify whether a training window is visible when us train.
                   The training window appears by default

showCommandLine  : Determine whether command-line output is generated.

show             : The number of epochs between command-line feedback during training.

> The training window appears automatcally when we train.
  Use these functions to manually apon and close the training window :

        nntraintool
        nntraintool('close')
